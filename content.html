Reason

For security reasons, we only accept cash payments. This ensures that transactions remain safe, private, and free from potential online fraud or chargeback issues. By using cash, we eliminate the risk of stolen credit card information, unauthorized transactions, and processing fees that could increase costs for our customers. Our goal is to provide a secure and straightforward payment process that protects both our business and our valued customers.
